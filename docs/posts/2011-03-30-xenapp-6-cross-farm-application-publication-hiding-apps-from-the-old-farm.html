XenApp 6 Cross Farm Application Publication - Hiding Apps from the old farm

2011-03-30
citrixxenapp-6

To hide applications from the other farm within the online plug-in I have used this article http://support.citrix.com/article/CTX123969 - basically add a #(hash) character in the Application description field - this way you can cross publish apps without getting a crazy large list of apps from both farms for users!
