Welcome to emailtest.org - the easy way to test that your e-mail is working.

Simply send an e-mail to test@emailtest.org from your e-mail client and you should receive a reply back. If you do then your e-mail has gone from your network, out through the internet to our server, been processed and sent back to you - proving that your e-mail is working!

If you have any queries, then please e-mail admin@emailtest.org.
