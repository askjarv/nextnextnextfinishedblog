It's been an exciting few months in the world of Microsoft - a new Server OS, a new Lync server, a new Exchange server, a new client OS (with multiple variants), a new Sharepoint etc. - it's getting hard for even the die hard like myself to keep up - so I've decided to dive in and start a series of posts detailing a lab setup for installing each of these products. It's not aimed as a definitive "how to", or even aimed at showing how to put together a good test lab- but rather a chance to explore the new features and developments and see what can be achieved quickly and easily- I hope you enjoy the journey with me!
